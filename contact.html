Frequently Asked Questions

Do you ship internationally?

Yes, we ship our products worldwide. Shipping costs and delivery times vary by destination. Please contact us for specific information about shipping to your country.

What is the minimum order for wholesale?

Our minimum wholesale order is 20 liters or 24 bottles (500ml). We offer special pricing for larger quantities. Please contact our wholesale department for a customized quote.

How should I store olive oil?

Olive oil should be stored in a cool, dark place away from heat and light. Avoid storing near the stove or in direct sunlight. Properly stored, our olive oil maintains its quality for up to 24 months from the production date.

Do you offer private labeling?

Yes, we offer private labeling services for businesses looking to create their own branded olive oil products. We can accommodate custom packaging, labeling, and formulations based on your requirements.
