Our Premium Olive Oils

Extra Virgin Olive Oil

Acidity

< 0.8%

Harvest

Early Season

Flavor

Robust, Fruity

Our flagship product, Extra Virgin Olive Oil, is the highest quality olive oil available. It's produced by cold-pressing fresh olives without any heat or chemicals, preserving the natural flavors, aromas, and nutritional benefits. With its low acidity level (less than 0.8%) and exceptional taste, it's perfect for dressings, dips, and finishing dishes.

Health Benefits

  • Rich in antioxidants
  • High in heart-healthy monounsaturated fats
  • Contains anti-inflammatory compounds
  • Supports brain health

Virgin Olive Oil

Acidity

< 2%

Harvest

Mid Season

Flavor

Balanced, Smooth

Virgin Olive Oil is also produced by mechanical means only, without any chemical treatment, but has a slightly higher acidity level (less than 2%). It offers a milder flavor profile while still retaining most of the nutritional benefits of extra virgin olive oil. Ideal for everyday cooking, sautéing, and baking.

Culinary Uses

  • Perfect for medium-heat cooking
  • Ideal for marinades and sauces
  • Great for baking and pastries
  • Excellent for everyday use

Refined Olive Oil

Acidity

< 0.3%

Process

Refined

Flavor

Mild, Neutral

Refined Olive Oil is processed from virgin olive oils using methods to remove impurities while preserving the oil's structure. It has a very mild flavor and high smoke point, making it excellent for high-heat cooking methods like frying and roasting. Our refining process maintains the oil's stability while minimizing flavor loss.

Cooking Benefits

  • High smoke point (240°C/465°F)
  • Neutral flavor profile
  • Ideal for deep frying
  • Perfect for recipes requiring no olive flavor

Olive Pomace Oil

Source

Olive Residue

Process

Extracted & Refined

Usage

Commercial

Olive Pomace Oil is extracted from the olive pulp after the first press. This economical oil is perfect for commercial kitchens and food manufacturing. It has a high smoke point and neutral flavor, making it suitable for various cooking applications. Our sustainable approach ensures that we utilize every part of our olive harvest.

Commercial Applications

  • Cost-effective for restaurants
  • Stable in food manufacturing
  • Suitable for bulk cooking
  • Consistent performance in food products
