From Grove to Bottle

At Venezia Farms, we believe that exceptional olive oil begins with meticulous attention to every step of the production process. Our cold-pressing technique preserves the natural flavors, aromas, and nutritional benefits of our olives, resulting in premium quality oils that capture the essence of our Moroccan terroir.

Cultivation

Our olive groves span over 200 hectares of sun-drenched Moroccan hillsides, where the Mediterranean climate and rich soil create ideal conditions for olive cultivation. We grow several varieties of olives, each contributing unique characteristics to our oils.

  • Sustainable farming practices that respect the environment
  • Minimal use of pesticides and fertilizers
  • Traditional dry-farming techniques that enhance flavor concentration
  • Regular pruning to ensure optimal sunlight exposure and air circulation

Harvesting

The harvest season typically runs from October to December, with timing carefully determined by monitoring the olives' ripeness. We primarily harvest our olives by hand to ensure gentle handling and selection of only the best fruits.

  • Early harvest produces oils with more robust flavors and higher polyphenol content
  • Hand-picking prevents damage to both the fruit and the trees
  • Olives are transported to our mill in ventilated crates to prevent fermentation
  • Processing begins within hours of harvesting to preserve freshness

Cold-Pressing

The term "cold-pressed" refers to the extraction of olive oil at temperatures below 27°C (80°F). This gentle process preserves the oil's natural flavors, aromas, and nutritional properties that would otherwise be degraded at higher temperatures.

1. Cleaning

Olives are washed to remove leaves, twigs, and other debris.

2. Crushing

The olives are crushed into a paste using modern stainless steel mills.

3. Malaxation

The paste is slowly mixed to allow oil droplets to combine and separate from the solids.

4. Extraction

The paste is pressed or centrifuged to separate the oil from water and solids.

Filtration & Storage

After extraction, our olive oil undergoes minimal filtration to remove any remaining particles while preserving its natural characteristics. The oil is then stored in temperature-controlled stainless steel tanks to maintain freshness before bottling.

  • Gentle filtration preserves natural antioxidants and flavor compounds
  • Temperature-controlled storage prevents degradation
  • Nitrogen blanketing protects the oil from oxidation
  • Regular quality testing ensures consistency and excellence

Quality Assurance

At Venezia Farms, quality is our highest priority. Our oils undergo rigorous testing throughout the production process to ensure they meet international standards and our own exacting requirements.

Chemical Analysis

Testing for acidity, peroxide values, and other chemical parameters.

Sensory Evaluation

Expert tasting panels assess flavor, aroma, and overall quality.

Certification

Compliance with international standards and organic certifications.

Traceability

Complete tracking from grove to bottle for quality assurance.
