Our Story

A family tradition of excellence since 1932

A Rich Family Heritage

The story of Venezia Farms begins in 1932 when the Venezia family, with Italian roots and a passion for olive cultivation, established their first olive grove in the fertile hills of Morocco's Marrakech region. What started as a modest family operation has grown into one of Morocco's premier olive oil producers, while still maintaining the family values and artisanal approach that have defined us for generations.

For nearly a century, we have nurtured our olive trees with dedication and respect for the land. Each generation has contributed to our growth while preserving our commitment to quality and sustainability. Today, the third and fourth generations of the Venezia family continue this proud tradition, blending time-honored methods with modern innovations to create exceptional olive oils.

Our Journey Through Time

1932

The Beginning

Antonio Venezia plants the first olive trees on a 10-hectare plot in Morocco.

1948

First Mill

The family builds their first traditional olive mill, beginning small-scale oil production.

1965

Expansion

Second-generation leadership expands the groves to 50 hectares and modernizes production.

1982

50th Anniversary

Venezia Farms celebrates 50 years with the introduction of premium bottled olive oil.

1997

Modernization

Investment in state-of-the-art cold-pressing technology while maintaining traditional values.

2010

Organic Certification

Venezia Farms receives organic certification for a significant portion of its groves.

2025

Global Expansion

The family launches international exports, bringing Moroccan olive oil excellence to the world.

Our Core Values

Sustainability

We are committed to environmentally responsible farming practices that preserve our land for future generations. Our water conservation methods, minimal use of chemicals, and sustainable harvesting techniques reflect our deep respect for nature.

Quality

Excellence is non-negotiable at Venezia Farms. From cultivation to bottling, we maintain rigorous quality standards that exceed industry requirements. Our commitment to quality has earned us recognition in international olive oil competitions.

Community

We believe in supporting the local communities that have been part of our journey. Venezia Farms provides fair employment to over 100 local families and invests in community development projects throughout the Marrakech region.

Innovation

While honoring traditional methods, we continuously seek ways to improve our processes through innovation. Our research partnerships with agricultural universities help us stay at the forefront of sustainable olive cultivation.
